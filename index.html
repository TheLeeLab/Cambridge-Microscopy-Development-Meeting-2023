The Cambridge Microscopy Meeting is a meeting organised for and by microscopists in the Cambridge area. It will be held on Mon 9 and Tue 10 October 2023 at the Yusuf Hamied Department of Chemistry at the University of Cambridge.

Meeting dates 9-10 October 2023
